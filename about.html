Unyo Center for Sustainable Development Foundation

The Unyo Center for Sustainable Development Foundation (Unyo Centre) is a dynamic non-governmental organization founded by a coalition of visionary youths from diverse sectors united in their commitment to empowering the next generation. As a registered not-for-profit entity with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), we are dedicated to fostering youth capacity development and creating transformative opportunities for young people.

Our mission encompasses a broad spectrum of initiatives, including socio-economic empowerment, youth development and capacity building, health, education, research, civic engagement, and the empowerment of both youth and women. By equipping young individuals with the tools and resources they need, we enable them to drive sustainable growth for themselves and their communities.

At UCSDF, we believe in the power of youth to lead change and are committed to supporting their journey towards a sustainable and prosperous future.

our vision

Our Vision to Inspire, Empower, and transform

Our vision to reach young people.

We envision a world where every person, especially young people, has equal access to opportunities and the right information to lead productive and successful lives. Our mission is to empower individuals by providing them with the resources, knowledge, and support needed to shape their futures. We are dedicated to fostering an environment of equality, personal growth, and community empowerment, ensuring that every person has the tools to thrive and make a positive impact on society.

our mission

Our Mission to Serve, Love, and Grow

Our mission to reach young people.

Our mission is to empower young people to become catalysts for sustainable and progressive growth, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and opportunities needed to shape their future and uplift their communities. We are committed to fostering leadership, resilience, and innovation, ensuring that they not only thrive individually but also contribute meaningfully to positive societal transformation.
